About SwarmBase

SwarmBase is a next-generation cybersecurity platform designed to make threat detection, risk analysis, and vulnerability management simple, fast, and accessible.

Our mission is to give developers, businesses, and agencies the ability to scan, monitor, and secure their systems in real time without needing enterprise-level security teams.

What We Do

SwarmBase analyzes your domain for security risks, misconfigurations, and exposure points. We convert complex security data into clear, actionable insights so you know exactly what to fix.

Instead of overwhelming you with raw data, we provide:

• Risk scores you can understand
• Prioritized vulnerabilities
• Actionable fix recommendations
• Continuous monitoring (Pro & above)

Why SwarmBase Exists

Traditional security tools are built for large enterprises — expensive, complex, and difficult to use.

SwarmBase changes that by delivering enterprise-level insights in a simple interface that anyone can use.

Privacy First

We do NOT sell your data.

We do NOT share your scan results with third parties.

Your domains, findings, and security data remain private and under your control.
